About Papi's Pictures

Papi's Pictures is a Northern Michigan photography project capturing the natural beauty of the Great Lakes region through aerial drone and landscape photography. From the iconic Point Betsie Lighthouse to the hidden marshes of Arcadia, every image tells a story of Michigan's breathtaking coastline, dense pine forests, and golden sunsets over Lake Michigan.

Our mission is simple: share the beauty of Northern Michigan with the world. Whether you're a local who wants a piece of home on your wall, a business looking for stunning regional imagery, or a creative professional seeking unique content, Papi's Pictures has something for you. All images are available for personal and commercial licensing.

Have a question or want to book a custom photo shoot? Get in touch at contact@papispictures.com
